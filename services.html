Hardware/Board Design & Development

Caravel Systems Inc offers a broad spectrum of services in the areas of electronic design, embedded software, and hardware development.

  • Single Board Computers (SBC)

    Custom designs for high-performance SBCs tailored to specific industrial applications.

  • Industrial Motherboard Designs

    Development of robust, reliable motherboards for industrial environments.

  • Add-on Cards

    Design and development of add-on cards based on various bus architectures, including PCI, ISA, PC104, PCI104, and PCMCIA.

  • Peripheral Adapters

    Customized peripheral adapters for storage interfaces like IDE, SATA, SCSI, and Compact Flash.

  • Micro-controller Based Boards

    Design of standalone and handheld systems, as well as standalone and network-enabled industrial controllers.

  • DSP-based Controllers

    Development of digital signal processing (DSP) controllers for various processing and control applications.

  • Ruggedized Systems

    Designing rugged systems that meet military and industrial standards for demanding environments.

  • Silicon Validation and Reference Boards

    Offering services for silicon validation, including the creation of reference boards for new silicon designs.

  • Custom Built Test Jigs

    Development of specialized test jigs for product validation and testing.
