Product Design & Development

Comprehensive Expertise Under One Roof

At Caravel, we provide the unique advantage of combining hardware design, embedded software, and application software development under a single roof. This integrated approach ensures seamless collaboration and communication, resulting in faster development cycles and a more cohesive end product.

Collaborative Ecosystem for Rapid Product Realization

Caravel’s collaborations with top-tier manufacturing and testing partners create an exceptional ecosystem that supports the rapid development and deployment of your product. This ensures that your product is not only designed to meet your specifications but is also manufactured and tested to the highest standards.

End-to-End Product Development Management

Whether you require assistance at any single stage of the product development cycle or need end-to-end management of your product from concept to final delivery, Caravel has the experience and expertise to handle it.

Comprehensive Electronic Design Services

Caravel offers a full suite of electronic design services, including schematic capture, PCB layout, and design for manufacturability (DFM). Our team of experts ensures that your designs are not only functional but also optimized for cost and manufacturability.
