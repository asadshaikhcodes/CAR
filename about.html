Caravel Systems Inc

headquartered in San Jose, California, is at the forefront of providing innovative hardware and embedded software solutions, with a specialized focus on defense applications.

Our Vision

Engineering Excellence, from Idea to Impact

We at Caravel Systems Inc, offer a comprehensive suite of end-to-end product realization services, covering everything from hardware design and embedded software development to application software solutions. This integrated approach ensures that Caravel delivers high-quality, reliable, and efficient products tailored to meet the dynamic and complex needs of its customers, including those in the defense sector.

Our Team

The minds behind the mission, powering every breakthrough.

Founded by a team of highly qualified professionals with advanced degrees from renowned Indian institutions like the IITs and IISc, Caravel has been a trusted partner in the technology sector for over two decades. Their state-of-the-art development facility in Bangalore, India, is equipped with the latest tools and technologies to manage complex projects. Caravel serves a diverse range of Indian defense organizations, including ADE, DRDO, BEL, and ANURAG, ensuring the highest standards of quality, innovation, and reliability.

At the Frontline of Defense Tech Evolution
