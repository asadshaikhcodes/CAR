Our Pricing Models

Fixed Price Model

Ideal for Small-duration projects with well-defined requirements and minimal scope for change.

  • Scope:Suitable for projects where the specifications are fully defined, and there is no expected change or deviation.
  • Pricing:Once the scope and deliverables are agreed upon, Caravel estimates the required effort and offers a fixed price for the project.
  • Risk Mitigation:Caravel assumes the risk of any project overruns. If there are any unforeseen challenges or delays, the price remains unchanged.
  • Benefits:Provides clients with a clear and predictable budget from the outset, ensuring that costs remain fixed regardless of project challenges.

Time & Material Model

Ideal ForProjects with evolving requirements, ongoing updates, or prototyping.

  • Scope:Perfect for projects with evolving specifications, where continuous updates or iterative development is necessary (e.g., software that requires ongoing maintenance or prototyping).
  • Pricing:: The client and Caravel agree on a man-hour or man-month rate at the start. Resources are allocated as needed across various phases of the project.
  • Flexibility:: Resources can be ramped up or down based on the project’s progress, providing flexibility as the project evolves.
  • Project Management:A dedicated Caravel project manager (or the client’s own project manager) oversees the project, ensuring it remains on track and within scope.
  • Benefits:: Ideal for clients seeking flexibility and cost reduction, as they only pay for the resources they use, while Caravel manages the scalability of the team to meet project demands.

Cost Plus Model

Ideal For Clients looking to set up a Dedicated Overseas Development Center (ODC) for long-term projects or large-scale system/product development.

  • Scope:This model is best suited for clients with long-term development needs, such as building a complete system/product with ongoing maintenance or releasing new versions continuously.
  • Customization:: The client identifies their requirements for technical resources, space, and infrastructure. Caravel provides the necessary setup, including the selection of technical resources as per the client’s criteria.
  • Cost Structure:: Caravel charges a percentage-based overhead for the management of operations, which includes costs for technical resources, infrastructure, equipment depreciation, and other overheads.
  • Dedicated Management Management:A dedicated project manager ensures smooth management of operations at the ODC. The client has direct involvement in selecting resources.
  • Benefits:: This model offers significant cost reduction over time and provides the flexibility of a dedicated team with scalable operations. It’s ideal for clients wishing to reduce operational costs by transferring their IT development and maintenance overseas.
